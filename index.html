How ZenChain Works

Our innovative technology stack delivers unmatched performance and sustainability

1

Green Proof of Stake

Our modified Proof of Stake consensus mechanism eliminates energy waste while maintaining security and decentralization. Validators are selected based on their stake and environmental contribution.

2

Sharded Architecture

ZenChain uses horizontal scaling through sharding, allowing the network to process transactions in parallel across multiple chains while maintaining atomic composability.

3

Optimized Virtual Machine

The Zen Virtual Machine (ZVM) executes smart contracts with unprecedented efficiency, reducing computational overhead and energy consumption by up to 80%.

4

Carbon Offset Protocol

A portion of all transaction fees is automatically allocated to verified carbon offset projects, making every transaction on ZenChain carbon negative.
